Enchilada Sauce

Description

A delicious smoky sauce for the best enchiladas you'll ever taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin tomatoes
  • 1 to 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp miso. Or sub wit salt
  • 5 tsp chili powder
  • Â¼ chipotle powder or smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • Jalapenos to taste

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth
  2. Serve over enchiladas, nachos, whatever you fancy
