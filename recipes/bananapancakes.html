Banana Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are delicious. We had them this morning and have been smiling ever since

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch salt

Steps

  1. Preheat large frying pan
  2. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend til smooth
  3. Fry on high heat for 2-3 minutes, turning half way through
  4. Eat. Leave washing up for another day
