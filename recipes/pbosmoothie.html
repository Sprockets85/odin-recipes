Peanut Butter Oatmeal Smoothie

Description

Pretty self explanatory. It's a smoothie. I drink these a lot.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup of oats
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 whole banana
  • ½ cup of milk
  • 1 tsp chia seeds, optional

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth
  2. Pour into a jam jar, for some reason, and enjoy
